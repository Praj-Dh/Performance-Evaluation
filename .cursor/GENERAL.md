# Project: Performance Platform
> **Status:** Initial Development | **Deployment Target:** CATTLE (UB CSE Server)

## 🎯 Project Purpose
The **Performance Platform** is designed to eliminate bias and promote transparency in employee performance evaluations. By replacing sporadic annual reviews with a continuous, data-driven system, the platform ensures that evaluations are based on a longitudinal history of evidence rather than "recency bias" or subjective memory.

### Key Problems Solved:
* **Recency Bias:** Prevents the most recent month of work from overshadowing an entire year of contributions.
* **Invisible Work:** Provides a mechanism to log "glue work" like mentoring and peer support.
* **Subjectivity:** Uses weighted metrics and structured quality dimensions to standardize evaluations.

---

## 👥 User Personas & Workflows

### 1. Employees
* **Primary Goal:** Track personal growth and ensure contributions are visible to management.
* **Workflow:** * Log collaboration events (mentoring, knowledge sharing).
    * Monitor real-time performance scores and trends.
    * Review feedback history to align with company expectations.

### 2. Managers
* **Primary Goal:** Objective team management and data-backed promotion/compensation decisions.
* **Workflow:** * Create structured reviews using specific quality dimensions.
    * Compare direct reports across similar roles to ensure fairness.
    * Set and track individual performance goals.

---

## 🛠 Scope & Technical Environment
* **Platform Type:** Responsive Web Application.
* **Browser Support:** Optimized for Chrome and Firefox.
* **Infrastructure:** Deployed on **CATTLE** (University at Buffalo CSE department server).

---

## ✨ High-Level Features

### 🔹 For Employees
* **Employee Dashboard:** Visual representation of performance scores and contribution trends.
* **Performance History:** A versioned timeline of all past reviews and feedback.
* **Collaboration Log:** A dedicated form for tracking mentoring, peer support, and teamwork.
* **Score Breakdown:** Granular view of performance across quality dimensions.
* **Team Recognition:** A "Kudos" system for peer-to-peer appreciation.

### 🔹 For Managers
* **Manager Dashboard:** High-level overview of team health and performance summaries.
* **Structured Reviews:** Evaluation tool using defined dimensions (*Technical Excellence, Impact & Delivery, Leadership & Influence*).
* **Direct Report Management:** Filterable list of employees by job title, score, and review status.
* **Deep-Dive Employee Details:** Comprehensive views showing trends, logs, and historical data.
* **Goal Management:** Interface to set and track specific performance objectives.
* **Company Directory:** Global visibility of the organization's talent pool.

---

## 📊 Quality Dimensions
Evaluations are based on three primary pillars:
1. **Technical Excellence:** Quality of code, architectural decisions, and technical problem-solving.
2. **Impact & Delivery:** Reliability, meeting deadlines, and value added to the business.
3. **Leadership & Influence:** Mentorship, culture-building, and cross-team collaboration.
